# 🏓 PONG - Classic Two-Player Game

A modern web-based version of the classic Pong arcade game! Both players control their paddles simultaneously in real-time action.

## 🎮 Play Online

https://wtlj2026.github.io/pong

## ✨ Features

- 🎯 **Real-Time Simultaneous Gameplay** - Both players move at the same time
- ⌨️ **Keyboard Controls** - Easy to learn, responsive controls
- 🏆 **Score Tracking** - First to 11 points wins
- ⏸️ **Pause/Resume** - Take breaks whenever you need
- 📱 **Responsive Design** - Play on desktop, tablet, or mobile
- 🎨 **Beautiful Neon UI** - Modern cyberpunk-inspired design
- 🔗 **Easy Share** - Built-in link sharing for friends

## 🕹️ How to Play

### Controls

**Player 1 (Left Paddle - Blue)**
- `W` - Move paddle up
- `S` - Move paddle down

**Player 2 (Right Paddle - Purple)**
- `P` - Move paddle up
- `L` - Move paddle down

### Objective

1. Click **Start Game** to begin
2. Both players move their paddles simultaneously
3. Hit the ball back and forth
4. If the ball passes your paddle, your opponent scores a point
5. First player to reach **11 points** wins!
6. Use **Pause** to take a break anytime
