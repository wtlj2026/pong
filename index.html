How to Play

⬆️ Player 1 (Left Paddle)

W - Move Up

S - Move Down

⬇️ Player 2 (Right Paddle)

↑ Arrow - Move Up

↓ Arrow - Move Down

Rules

  • Both players move their paddles simultaneously in real-time
  • Hit the ball with your paddle to bounce it back
  • If the ball passes your paddle, your opponent scores
  • First to 11 points wins!
